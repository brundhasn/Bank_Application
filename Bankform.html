INTRODUCTION OF BB BANK

A BB bank is a financial institution where customers can save or borrow money.BB Banks also invest money to build up their reserve of money.The Folks who work at a bank are called bank employees. The BB bank directly deal with the public. Customer's money may be placed in the bank for safe keeping. BB Bank may give loans to customers under an agreement to pay the money back to the bank at a later time, with an interest. An example is getting a mortgage to buy a house or apartment. BB Bank also can use the money they have from deposit accounts to invest in businesses in order to make more money.
